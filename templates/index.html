About us

Some words about us

RAH Infotech was established in 2005 with a focus on providing state-of-the-art technology solutions in association with Global IT leaders. In over a decade of its operations, RAH Infotech has been consistently providing world class IT solutions to a large number of channel partners and corporates in India and SAARC countries. Headquartered in Gurgoan, RAH Infotech has presence across the country with International presence in Netherlands, Singapore, UAE, UK and USA.
