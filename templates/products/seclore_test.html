Seclore provides Advanced Enterprise Rights Management Solutions. It offers FileSecure, which enables organizations to control usage of information wherever it goes, within and outside of the organization’s boundaries; and provides ability to remotely enforce and audit who can view, edit, copy content, and screen capture files that help organizations to embrace external collaboration, file sharing, and the use of mobile devices. The company also offers a series of pre-built connectors that plug-in digital rights management to existing DLP, ECM, ERP, and mail/messaging solutions.
