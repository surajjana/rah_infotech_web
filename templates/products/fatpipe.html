Fat Pipe



Fat Pipe is the inventor and multiple patents holder of software-defined wide area networking (SD-WAN), reliability, security, and WAN Optimization products, specializes in providing solutions that transcend Wide Area Network (WAN) failures to maintain business continuity for thousands of customers including numerous Fortune 1000 customers over the last 12 years, and has the largest installed base of customers in software-defined network WANs.

  • Premise Managed Solutions - FatPipe Premise Managed Solutions enable central control of WAN from headquarters, easy management of branch appliances and zero-touch deployment.
  • Hosted Managed Solutions - FatPipe Hosted Solutions offer resiliency and continuity for businesses through Software Defined WANs with zero-touch deployment and hosted management and reporting.
  • FatPipe As A Service For MSPs - FatPipe empowers Managed Service Providers to offer managed hybrid WANs, intelligent multi path control, network visibility and reporting with WAN as a Service.
  • Hybrid Multi-Path Load Balancing - FatPipe WARP and MPVPN offer intelligent load balancing, high reliability and high security over hybrid WANs with a combination of MPLS and broadband lines.
  • Multi-Gigabit Solutions - FatPipe offers multi gigabit bandwidth support with multiple multi-gigabit broadband connections and millions of sessions.
  • Multi-Line WAN Optimization - FatPipe offers Multi-Line WAN Optimization and QoS with WAN redundancy & Internet Load Balancing for improved user experience,
  • MPSec – Multi-Path Security - FatPipe MPVPN enhances VPN security with MPSec providing world's highest level of security along with reliability and speed.
  • Application Control And Visibility - FatPipe EnterpriseView offers in-depth application and user visibility, control and reporting for SD-WANs.
  • Multi Site Reliability (Disaster Recovery) - FatPipe solutions for back-up and DR sites allow for data failover and resiliency for branch connectivity.
  • Hybrid Multi-Line WAN - A Hybrid Multiline WAN is a Wide Area Network using a combination of public and private data lines.
  • SD-WAN (Software-Defined WAN) - Simplify deployment and management of connectivity at remote sites. FatPipe Orchestrator™ easily controls and manages your WAN.
  • Avaya + FatPipe Integrated Solution - FatPipe and Avaya partner to simplify Software-Defined architecture everywhere, "Datacenter-to-Desktop" combining a fabric-based SDN with next generation hybrid, software-defined WAN (SD-WAN) solution.
